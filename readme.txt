== Description ==

1 Click SEO optimizes your individual posts or pages with just a click of a button and choosing your keyword for that page/post.  With just a simple click of a button, you will can instantly fully optimized your page or post and start to see your search engine rankings sky rocket overnight.

How it works:
1. Create a new Page or Post
2. In the right column, your will see a new panel called "One Click SEO Config"
3. The top line option is to optimize your page for the specific keyword by adding the H1, H2, and if needed, and Image Alt tag. (If there is no image, don't check the "Image Alt" or you might get an error).
4. In the bottom line, you can choose to include a link to the homepage, link to post, bold, underline, and italics.

Once you have configured your options, you can now choose a main keyword that is found in your Post or Page and the click "Apply Changes"

You will then notice that you now have an H1 and H2 tag in the Post/Page, and the keyword that is found in your article now has a link to the homepage, a link to that article page, a bolded keyword, an underlined keyword, and an italic keyword.

You can see a demo at: oneclick.iintense.com

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

e.g.

1. Upload `one-click-seo` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Use 1 Click SEO by creating or editing a page or post.
